 MATTERHORN
Helicopter sightseeing flight Matterhorn
Description

From Buochs, glide over Obwalden towards Brünig. Along the impressive Eiger North Face, the majestic backdrop of Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau unfolds. The flight continues over the imposing Aletsch Glacier towards Aletschhorn, Matter Valley, and Zermatt.

The highlight is the breathtaking view of the Matterhorn, which you can optionally admire at the mountain landing site "Unterrothorn." An unforgettable experience that celebrates the beauty of the Swiss Alps from above.

 JUNGFRAUJOCH
Helicopter sightseeing flight Jungfraujoch
Description

Experience an unforgettable flight from Buochs Airfield in the picturesque canton of Nidwalden. The journey takes you over Grindelwald, along the world-famous Eiger North Face, between the Mönch and Jungfrau, to the glacier mountain landing site at Jungfraujoch.

Guided by the pilot, take a few steps to Jungfraujoch Station, where you can enjoy the distinctive view of the impressive Aletsch Glacier.

After a 1.5-hour stay, fly back to Buochs, carrying beautiful memories and impressions of an unforgettable excursion.

Book now for a journey filled with breathtaking landscapes and unparalleled experiences!
